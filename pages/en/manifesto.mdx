# Manifesto

<Steps>
### <span className="text-pink-500">**Transparency**</span> before <span className="text-blue-500">**Profitability**</span>

I pledge absolute transparency in my dealings. No hidden costs, no deceptive UX, no unauthorized data selling. Respect for my users or customers is paramount.

### <span className="text-pink-500">**Quality**</span> before <span className="text-blue-500">**Quantity**</span>

I will be driven by passion for my product. I will prioritize exceptional user experience, and focus on delivering fewer, but superior solutions.

### <span className="text-pink-500">**Inclusivity**</span> before <span className="text-blue-500">**Opinions**</span>

I will stand for an unbiased, inclusive space. I will welcome diverse perspectives, foster freedom, and remain politically neutral, creating spaces for all.

### <span className="text-pink-500">**Communication**</span> before <span className="text-blue-500">**Selfishness**</span>

I will be open to feedback and constructive criticism. I will share my knowledge to uplift everyone, fostering a community of support and collaboration.

</Steps>

I, **[Your name]**, pledge to uphold these values and principles.
